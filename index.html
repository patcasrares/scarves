LYGIA
HAND PAINTED SILK

Lygia is an artist with special sensitivity, specialized in silk painting in Italy, where the sense of beauty and art finds an ideal environment. Her art pieces are a mirroring of many years of experience and dedication to a fascinating domain. She exalts the beauty of this wonderful natural material with deep respect for its characteristics: softness, enveloping, and pleasantness to the touch. Every piece is unique, result of own inspiration , made with unbelievable feelings during the execution. Part of those vibes, mixed with a big dose of positivity is ready to be a gift , or a little delight in a world full of colors.
